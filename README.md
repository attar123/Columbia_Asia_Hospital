## Key Insights

### 1. Revenue & Department Performance  
- Identified top-earning departments and underperforming units.  
- Uncovered revenue disparities across service lines.

### 2. Wait Time vs. Patient Satisfaction  
- Demonstrated a strong inverse correlation between wait times and satisfaction scores.  
- High-traffic departments showed greater risk of dissatisfaction.

### 3. Demographic Trends  
- Segmented patients by age groups to uncover utilization patterns.  
- Enabled more targeted care planning and outreach.

### 4. Seasonal Visit Patterns  
- Identified predictable visit spikes during specific months.  
- Supported capacity planning and staffing decisions.

### 5. Departmental Traffic Flow  
- Tracked referral and routing paths between departments.  
- Mapped high-congestion areas for workflow optimization.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- **Doctor Shift Realignment**  
  Adjust physician availability in high-traffic areas to reduce congestion and improve response time.

- **Smart Discounting Models**  
  Design data-driven pricing and discount strategies to retain patients without hurting revenue.

- **Demographic-Based Engagement**  
  Use age and behavior segmentation to deliver personalized communication and promotions.

- **Seasonal Resource Allocation**  
  Prepare staffing and supplies ahead of expected visit peaks for smoother operations.

- **Focused Departmental Investment**  
  Prioritize upgrades in high-performing departments to scale patient throughput and satisfaction.
